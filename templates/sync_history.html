About Sync History

This page shows all automatic data synchronizations triggered by Salsify webhooks. Each time you publish your product feed in Salsify, a webhook is sent to this application, and the database is updated automatically.

  • Products Added: New products that didn't exist in the database
  • Products Updated: Existing products with changed data
  • Products Deleted: Products removed from Salsify
  • Compatibilities Updated: Number of compatibility matches recalculated
